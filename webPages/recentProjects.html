Blackjack Express

Blackjack Express is a Blackjack inspired game that was built using Vanilla JS. To win the game, you must get exactly 21! The default bet is 10 chips, and before a round starts you can raise or lower the bet. When the round is finished, your total number of chips will either increase or decrease depending on if you won or lost the game! This project was really fun to build and I am still working on adding more functionality.
